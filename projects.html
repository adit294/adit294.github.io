Twitter bots that notify about vaccine availabiltiy in 6 major Indian cities

Bot works throughout the day and tweets when vaccines appointments are available. Book an appointment at a center near you ASAP. Remember to switch on notifications! Link to other cities:-https://t.co/Yk2KNvV3NE

— Mumbai Covid Vaccine Bot (@CovidvaxBOM) May 22, 2021

Due to shortage of vaccines in India, it has been very difficult to book slots online. This bot was developed using python and has received over half a million impressions and 1200+ followers. We continue to take feedback and add new features.
