How to Play

Level 1 – Falling Roses: Move Cupid 💘 with Arrow keys or WASD (or on-screen pad on mobile). Catch falling roses 🌹 and avoid the clouds. Collect 8 to win!

Level 2 – Chocolate Catch: Move left/right only at the bottom. Catch falling chocolates 🍫 and avoid the rain. Collect 10 to win!

Level 3 – Cupid’s Arrow: Aim with the mouse (or touch), then click/tap to shoot. Hit the falling hearts 💖 to collect 8—don’t hit the broken hearts 💔 or you lose a life! Then break the barriers and hit the jackpot to win.

You have 3 hearts (lives) in levels 1–2. Complete all 3 levels to reveal the surprise!
